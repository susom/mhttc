The MHTTC Shared Folder

The MHTTC Shared Folder is the main folder available to all MHTTC Staff, containing information on working groups, GPRA, the TTC Hub, materials for new staff members, and more! Access the whole Shared Folder or check out these Quick Links to some of the most popular resources:

  • Branding materials

    • Including MHTTC logos and Center images, website maintenance tips, and more

  • GPRA Documents/Resources

    • This folder contains information on how to utilize GPRA for your events, including generating event codes and creating language for post-event and follow-up surveys.

  • Instructional Design and Course Development on HealtheKnowledge

  • MHTTC and TTC Groups

    • This folder contains information on the 5 Working Groups and 5 Coordination Groups within the MHTTC Network, and the 6 groups across the TTC Network that the MHTTC Network is a part of.

  • MHTTC and TTC Guidance Documents

  • MHTTC Network Coordinating Office Trainings

  • MHTTC New Staff Orientation

  • MHTTC Reports

  • MHTTC Staff Roster

    • This list contains the names and emails for all MHTTC Centers across the country.

  • Notes from previous MHTTC Meetings

    • This folder contains notes from previous MHTTC All Staff and Directors Meetings

  • School Mental Health Initiative information

  • And more!
